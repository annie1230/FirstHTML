What are the environmental consequences of computer device production?

Habitat Destruction

When creating computer devices, multiple natural resources are needed. This causes humans to extract them from nature, where many living things call home. Lots of living things have their home broken or are forced to move away somewhere else because humans are extracting resources from their home to create computers.
